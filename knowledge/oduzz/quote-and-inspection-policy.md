# Oduzz Quote and Inspection Policy

Oduzz uses a practical quote process that balances speed with technical accuracy. Early chat or WhatsApp guidance can provide direction, but final recommendations depend on complete project details and, where needed, site inspection.

## What helps Oduzz prepare faster quotes

- Service type (solar, wiring, lighting, CCTV, maintenance, etc.).
- Project location.
- Current issue or installation goal.
- Timeline and urgency.
- Photos/videos of existing setup.
- Load list for solar/inverter requests.

## Inspection requirement

Some projects need physical verification before final scope and pricing. This is especially important for rewiring, panel upgrades, hybrid solar systems, and safety-critical corrections.

## Pricing and variation

Preliminary guidance is not a final invoice. Pricing may change based on material grade, quantity, site condition, access limitations, and protection requirements discovered during assessment.

## Client handoff channel

WhatsApp is recommended for quote handoff because it supports quick clarification and image sharing. Oduzz uses this channel to finalize details and schedule next actions.
