# Oduzz Safety Policy for Electrical Guidance

Safety is a core rule in every Oduzz recommendation. Guidance is designed to reduce risk, protect property, and prevent avoidable faults.

## Non-negotiable safety principles

- No unsafe bypasses or improvised neutral/earth handling.
- No overloaded breaker recommendations.
- No unverified DIY wiring instructions for hazardous scenarios.
- No approval of counterfeit or visibly poor-grade materials.

## When to escalate immediately

Clients should seek immediate qualified electrician support when there is burning smell, sparks, repeated breaker trips, buzzing panels, overheating sockets, exposed live conductors, or water near electrical points.

## Role of online guidance

Chat guidance is a planning aid, not a substitute for on-site inspection. Final recommendations for protection devices, cable sizing, panel arrangement, and inverter/solar architecture require professional review.

## Customer direction

For urgent safety issues, the safest path is immediate professional intervention and direct WhatsApp or phone escalation to the Oduzz team.
