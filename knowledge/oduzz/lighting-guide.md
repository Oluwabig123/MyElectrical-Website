# Oduzz Lighting Design and Installation Guide

Lighting should improve visibility, comfort, and interior value. Oduzz supports functional and decorative lighting projects for homes, offices, shops, and hospitality spaces.

## Lighting service scope

- General lighting for balanced room brightness.
- Accent and feature lighting for aesthetics.
- POP and chandelier support with proper anchoring.
- Outdoor/security lighting planning.
- Switch-zone planning for comfort and energy control.

## Planning inputs

- Room purpose and expected activity pattern.
- Ceiling type and fixture placement constraints.
- Preferred color temperature and mood direction.
- Number of control zones required.

## Installation best practices

- Match fixture type to intended brightness and beam behavior.
- Use safe mounting and proper support for heavy fixtures.
- Separate zones for layered lighting control.
- Ensure clean wiring and accessible maintenance points.

## Important note

Final fixture recommendations and wiring strategy depend on space layout and inspection findings. Oduzz confirms final configuration after reviewing the project details or conducting a site visit.
