# Oduzz Frequently Asked Questions

## Do you serve only Ikorodu?

Oduzz is strongly active in Ikorodu and Lagos, and can support nearby areas based on project scope.

## Can I get a solar size estimate online?

Yes, preliminary guidance is possible with your load list, backup hours, and location. Final configuration still requires professional validation.

## Do you use authentic materials?

Yes. Oduzz prioritizes authentic electrical materials and avoids low-grade or fake components that increase fault risk.

## Can you give exact prices instantly?

Exact pricing depends on scope, material choices, quantities, and site condition. Share details for a structured quote.

## Do I need a site inspection?

Many projects can start with remote review, but complex jobs usually require site inspection before final recommendations and pricing.

## What details should I send for a quick quote?

Send service type, location, urgency, project details, and photos or load list where relevant.

## Can I continue on WhatsApp?

Yes. WhatsApp is the fastest way to hand over project details and receive quote-ready follow-up.
