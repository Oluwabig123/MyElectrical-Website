# Oduzz Electrical Concepts Company Profile

Oduzz Electrical Concepts is an electrical and energy solutions company serving Ikorodu, Lagos, and nearby areas. The company supports homeowners, landlords, retail businesses, offices, and project developers with practical electrical systems that prioritize safety, performance, and long-term reliability.

## What Oduzz focuses on

- Professional electrical installation for new and existing properties.
- Solar and inverter planning, installation, and upgrade support.
- Wiring and rewiring for homes, offices, and light commercial spaces.
- Lighting design support, fixture installation, and control setup.
- CCTV and smart-home integration with clean cable management.
- Troubleshooting and maintenance for common electrical faults.

## Service approach

Oduzz follows a clear process: understand the client need, inspect the site where required, recommend safe and realistic options, and execute with quality materials and neat finishing. Every recommendation is guided by load requirements, usage pattern, and operating environment.

## Service area and positioning

Oduzz is strongly positioned in Ikorodu and Lagos, with support for surrounding communities based on project scope. Response quality is built around fast communication, practical advice, and clear quote preparation.

## Customer communication

For quicker quote turnaround, clients are encouraged to share project location, service type, timeline, photos, and key details through WhatsApp. This helps the Oduzz team prepare better recommendations before site mobilization.

## Policy note

Final technical sizing, installation scope, and pricing are confirmed after professional review and, where necessary, a physical site inspection.
