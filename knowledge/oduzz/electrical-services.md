# Oduzz Electrical Services Guide

Oduzz Electrical Concepts provides structured electrical services for residential and light commercial properties in Ikorodu and Lagos. Services are delivered with attention to safety, load balance, equipment protection, and tidy workmanship.

## Core services

- Full electrical installation for new builds and renovations.
- Rewiring, conduit correction, and cable replacement for aging systems.
- Distribution board setup, breaker organization, and circuit separation.
- Socket, switch, and lighting-point installation for interior and exterior spaces.
- Earthing and surge protection integration where applicable.
- Fault finding for tripping, overheating, flickering, and intermittent outages.

## Typical client outcomes

- Reduced nuisance tripping and overloaded circuits.
- Better appliance protection and cleaner power distribution.
- Safer wiring routes and properly rated protective devices.
- Improved future expandability for AC units, pumps, lighting, and inverter systems.

## When to request a service assessment

Request an assessment when your property has recurring breaker trips, hot sockets, buzzing panels, dimming lights under load, frequent extension-cable use, or unclear wiring history. These are common indicators that the existing system needs professional review.

## Project readiness checklist

Before requesting a quote, share:

- Location and property type.
- Current electrical challenge or project goal.
- Urgency and preferred timeline.
- Photos or short videos of existing panels, wiring points, and affected areas.

Site inspection may be required for final recommendations, final scope, and final pricing.
