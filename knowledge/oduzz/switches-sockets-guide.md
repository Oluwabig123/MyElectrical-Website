# Oduzz Switches and Sockets Guide

Switches and sockets affect everyday safety, convenience, and finishing quality. Oduzz helps clients choose suitable options for both residential comfort and light commercial durability.

## Recommendation focus

- Correct type for each room and load behavior.
- Reliable contact quality and proper fitting.
- Clean alignment, plate finishing, and practical positioning.
- Compatibility with child-safety and smart-control requirements.

## Planning considerations

- Number of users and appliance density in each space.
- Dedicated outlets for heavy appliances.
- Moisture-prone zones that require additional protection.
- Future expansion for workstations, entertainment, or office equipment.

## Installation quality factors

- Secure cable terminations and correct polarity.
- Proper back-box depth and strain management.
- No overloaded multi-plug dependency for permanent use.

## Policy note

Exact brand availability and pricing are confirmed during active quote preparation. Oduzz prioritizes authentic materials and avoids unsafe low-grade accessories that compromise reliability.
