# Oduzz Protection and Distribution Guide

A safe electrical system depends on proper protection and organized distribution. Oduzz helps clients set up boards and protective devices that reduce fault risk and improve system stability.

## Coverage area

- Distribution board arrangement and labeling.
- Breaker selection aligned with circuit load.
- Surge protection where risk profile requires it.
- Circuit grouping for easier troubleshooting and maintenance.
- Upgrade planning for future loads and equipment.

## Core outcomes

- Better fault isolation when issues happen.
- Lower risk of cable overheating and overload damage.
- Cleaner electrical architecture for ongoing maintenance.

## Common warning signs

- Frequent tripping without clear cause.
- Hot panel surfaces or discolored breaker points.
- Mixed heavy and light loads on poorly grouped circuits.
- Repeated temporary bypass behavior.

## Safety policy

Oduzz does not support unsafe bypasses, overloaded breaker practices, or improvised protection setups. Final protection recommendations are verified through professional assessment before implementation.
