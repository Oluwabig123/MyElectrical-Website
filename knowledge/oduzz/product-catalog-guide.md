# Oduzz Product and Catalog Guidance

Oduzz helps customers choose practical electrical and energy materials that match project needs instead of buying random parts. Product guidance is aligned with expected load, installation context, and durability requirements.

## Product support areas

- Cables and wires for domestic and light commercial circuits.
- Switches, sockets, and accessories for interior finishing.
- Lighting products for functional and decorative applications.
- Protection and distribution components for safer power control.
- Solar/inverter accessories for backup and hybrid systems.
- CCTV and smart-home accessories for monitoring and automation.

## Selection approach

- Match product category to intended use and load behavior.
- Prioritize authentic, traceable materials from trusted brands.
- Verify compatibility across panels, breakers, accessories, and fixtures.
- Avoid underspecified or fake materials that increase failure risk.

## Stock, price, and specification policy

Price, stock availability, and exact technical specifications can change. Oduzz confirms these details during quote preparation or direct product inquiry. Customers should always request current confirmation before final purchase decisions.

## Best way to request product recommendations

Share your project type, service goal, expected load, and location. For replacements, include clear photos of existing devices or labels so the Oduzz team can suggest the most compatible option.
