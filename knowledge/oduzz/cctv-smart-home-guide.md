# Oduzz CCTV and Smart Home Guide

Oduzz provides practical CCTV and smart-home guidance for residential and light commercial properties. The goal is dependable coverage, stable power, and clean installation.

## CCTV planning essentials

- Coverage goals for entry points, perimeter, and key indoor zones.
- Camera placement that avoids blind spots.
- Reliable power routing and backup strategy.
- Cable discipline for neatness and long-term serviceability.

## Smart-home support areas

- Smart switches and control points.
- Basic automation planning for lighting and convenience.
- Integration-minded wiring preparation.

## Installation quality factors

- Structured cable routes and proper labeling.
- Secure device mounting and clean terminations.
- Backup-aware planning for critical monitoring points.

## Important note

Exact device recommendations depend on property layout, risk profile, and client budget. Oduzz confirms final equipment options, quantities, and scope after project review.

For quick support, share your location, property type, and what you want to monitor or automate.
