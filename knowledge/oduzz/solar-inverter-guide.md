# Oduzz Solar and Inverter Planning Guide

Oduzz supports clients who want reliable backup power and solar energy systems designed for real usage. Proper sizing is based on load profile, backup hours, battery strategy, and installation environment.

## Planning inputs Oduzz needs

- Critical appliances and their estimated wattage.
- Preferred backup duration during outage periods.
- Daytime vs night-time usage pattern.
- Installation location and shading constraints.
- Budget direction and upgrade expectations.

## Practical sizing principles

- Inverter selection includes headroom for startup surge and future additions.
- Battery capacity is matched to backup duration and acceptable depth of discharge.
- Solar array sizing is based on daily energy demand plus system losses.
- Protection devices and cable sizing must match current and voltage requirements.

## Common use cases

- Freezer plus lighting backup.
- Home office + internet + essential circuits.
- Small shop or office critical-load continuity.
- Hybrid setups for grid + inverter + solar contribution.

## Important policy

Any sizing shared online is preliminary guidance. Final design, battery configuration, panel arrangement, and protection architecture are confirmed after professional review and, where needed, site inspection.
