# Start before ceilings and finishes close your options

Electrical mistakes in new buildings often come from late decisions. Once finishing starts, routing changes become expensive and usually messy.

## Core checklist before first-fix

1. Confirm room-by-room load priorities.
2. Define dedicated circuits for heavy appliances.
3. Agree on distribution board position and capacity.
4. Plan conduit routes and access points clearly.

## Checklist before accessories and lighting

1. Confirm switch and socket positions against furniture layout.
2. Separate ambient, task, and accent lighting controls.
3. Verify breaker map labels before closing panels.
4. Test continuity and protection behavior under expected load.

## Handover checklist

- Keep breaker schedule documentation.
- Keep product and batch references for key materials.
- Keep a simple maintenance note for future upgrades.
