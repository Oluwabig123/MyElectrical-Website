# Focus on performance before fixture style

A beautiful fixture can still underperform if beam direction, spacing, and control zones are wrong.

## What to evaluate first

- Purpose of the space: task, ambient, or accent use.
- Target brightness and comfort level.
- Ceiling condition and installation constraints.

## Product selection points

- Prefer consistent color temperature across connected spaces.
- Confirm fitting compatibility with available drivers and dimmers.
- Balance feature lights with practical base illumination.

## Layout and control points

Separate controls for general, feature, and task lighting. This gives better flexibility and avoids over-lighting.

## Final quality check

After installation, review glare, dark spots, and switch grouping in real evening usage before final acceptance.
